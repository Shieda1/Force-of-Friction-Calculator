What is the force of friction?
Force of friction is the force that is generated by two surfaces that contact and slide against each other. THe force of friction is affected by the surface texture and the amount of force impelling them together.
How to calculate force of friction?
To calculate force of friction you should use the following formula:
f = µ * n
where:
f = friction force.
µ = coefficient of friction
N = normal force
Why use force of friction?
The force of friction is used in order to shlow thins down and stop stationary things from moving.
